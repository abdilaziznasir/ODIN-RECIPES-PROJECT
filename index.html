Recipes Title

    • MY FAVORITE FOOD FOR EVER

    Ingredients

    • bonless, skinless chicken breast
    • tablespoon olive oil
    • salt and papper to test
    • ooptionla sessoning of your test eg gerlic powder

    Steps to make chicken breast

    1. preheat the oven 400'F (200'c)
    2. both side of the chicken breasts with salt, papper, and any
      other you prefer
    3. Heat the olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
    4. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook
      for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
    5. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake
      for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is
      cooked through and no longer pink in the center.
    6. Remove the skillet from the oven and let
      the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving.
    7. Slice the chicken breasts and serve
      with your favorite side dishes.
